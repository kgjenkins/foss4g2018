News

Thanks for attending!

Wednesday, May 16, 2018 - 12:18

Thank you to our attendees, sponsors, speakers, and committee members! FOSS4G NA 2018 was a great conference because of you.

Please help us with one last thing... your thoughts on how we did. Please complete this brief survey so we can use your suggestions to make the next event even better.

To encourage you to take the survey, we are giving away a $100 gift certificate to Amazon.com. Enter by leaving your email address when completing the last question.

Machine Learning After Party

Wednesday, May 9, 2018 - 10:56

Enjoy an evening of networking on machine learning, computer vision, and the fast-growing world of open source projects that are making it easier than ever to apply these techniques in your own work and research.

The Machine Learning After Party features lightning talks, drinks, and snacks. Thanks to Climate Corporation and Azavea for hosting the event with support from Astraea and DevelopmentSeed.

  • Tuesday, May 15, 7 - 8:30pm
  • Lindbergh Room (Lobby Level), Hilton St. Louis at the Ballpark
  • Sign up here

Sunday Registration Means No Waiting!

Friday, May 4, 2018 - 00:49

If you arrive at the conference before 7pm on Sunday, you can pick up your badge then, and sleep in a bit later on Monday morning.

Onsite Registration Hours

  • Sunday: 16:00 – 19:00
  • Monday: 8:00 – 18:00
  • Tuesday: 8:00 – 17:00
  • Wednesday: 8:30 – 17:00

Hotel Rate Expires Soon, so Book Now!

Thursday, April 19, 2018 - 15:21

Be sure to book your stay ASAP at the Hilton St. Louis at the Ballpark. The special conference rate will go away soon.

All Monday to Wednesday daytime activities are at the hotel, and the Wednesday evening Gala Reception is just across the street.
